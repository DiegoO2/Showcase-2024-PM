About Us

What is Jurassic Park

Jurassic Park is a revolutionary theme park home to the origins of earth. Customers get to experience real dinosaurs which once roamed this earth over a million years ago! You and your loved ones can do things such as petting a dinosaur, riding one, and feed one! Our scientists have spent decades recreating the DNA of these creatures, and we have been able to reproduce 15 different species! Creatures such as the Tyrannosaurus, Triceratops, and Diplodocus are all present at this park!

Our head scientist, Henry Wu has spent years perfecting how to bring dinosaurs to life, and with Jurassic Park, he has be able to accomplish that goal.

Dennis Nedry has been coding since he was a child, with the skills he has developed over the years, he is a proud member of InGen and is our lead programmer.

John Hammond is the man that has started this park. Our CEO had one idea in mind while creating this park, give the people dinosaurs. With our crew, dinosaurs were able to come to life.

Ed Regis is our Public Relations manager and is dedicated to helping customers with any issues that come up with in our company.

Nate Sitthi was into dinosaurs since he as a kid. Ever since InGen opened up a position, he was quick to sign up and is working as a Park Associate here at Isla Nublar.
